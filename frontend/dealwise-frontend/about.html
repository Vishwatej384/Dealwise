About DealWise

DealWise began as a student project to make smart shopping easier and more fun. We aggregate verified deals, provide price history tools and surface honest user reviews to help you buy confidently.

  • Smart deal discovery across multiple sources
  • Price tracking & alerts
  • Trustworthy community reviews

Mission

Make shopping transparent and affordable for everyone through curated deals and helpful tools.

Team

A small team of web & backend students building practical, polished tools to help shoppers.
